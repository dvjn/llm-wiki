## Summary

The overview page for agentskills.io. Frames the value proposition from three angles: skill authors (build once, deploy across agents), compatible agents (extensibility out of the box), and teams/enterprises (organizational knowledge in portable packages). Lists four capability categories: domain expertise, new capabilities, repeatable workflows, and interoperability. Notes that the format was originally developed by Anthropic, released as an open standard, and is now adopted by ~35+ agent products.

## Key Extracts

> "Skills solve this by giving agents access to procedural knowledge and company-, team-, and user-specific context they can load on demand."

> "The Agent Skills format was originally developed by Anthropic, released as an open standard, and has been adopted by a growing number of agent products."

## Key Insights

- **Three distinct audiences**: the format is designed to benefit skill authors, agent implementors, and enterprise teams simultaneously — each stakeholder gets a different value proposition.
- **Organizational knowledge capture**: the enterprise pitch ("capture organizational knowledge in portable, version-controlled packages") is a direct parallel to Karpathy's compounding-wiki thesis applied at team scale.
- **Open standard framing**: originally Anthropic-developed but positioned as a community standard — broad adoption (35+ agents) validates this.
